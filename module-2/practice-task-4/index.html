Fuchka (Puchka)

Fuchka is one of the most popular street foods, especially in South Asia. It is made with crispy hollow shells filled with mashed potatoes, chickpeas, spices, and tamarind water. The spicy and tangy taste of fuchka makes it a favorite snack among people of all ages. It is commonly found at roadside stalls and enjoyed as a quick, flavorful treat.

Chotpoti

Chotpoti is a spicy and savory street food made from boiled peas, potatoes, eggs, onions, and a special blend of spices. It is usually served hot and topped with green chilies and tamarind sauce. Chotpoti is loved for its rich taste and strong aroma, making it a popular choice for evening snacks on the streets.
